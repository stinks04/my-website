Hobbies

Some of my favorite ways to pass the time or relax is by cooking some comfort foods. In this photo I cooked some chicken sausage, potatoes, and broccoli. Exploring Campus

After being here for over three years there is still so much I have yet to discover on campus. So I like to take walks around Campus to see if I find anything cool like a new coffee place, study spot, but my favorite place to walk through is the lakeshore path.
